The SAT and Race

The Facts don't lie!

  • Black and Hispanic/Latino students scored on average, over 100 points fewer than White students and over 200 points fewer than Asian students.

  • The gap is most dramatic on the math portion of the SAT, where less than 1/3 of Hispanic/Latino students and 1/4 of Black students meet the benchmark (over 1/2 of White students and 4/5 of Asian students meet this benchmark)

  • Of students scoring above a 700 on the SAT math, only 6% are Hispanic/Latino and a staggering 1% of students are Black

Source: National Center for Education Statistics, 2023
